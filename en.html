    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    What is Alicia's Calculator?

    Alicia's Calculator is an online math calculator designed for educational purposes. Unlike traditional calculators, it shows the step-by-step procedure of operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division, simulating a school blackboard.

    How do I use Alicia's Calculator?

    Simply enter the numbers using the interactive keypad or your own, select the desired mathematical operation, and press the equals button (=). The step-by-step procedure will be displayed above the final result.

    Is it free?

    Yes, it is a 100% free educational tool that will always be available to help students of all levels.

    Does it work on mobile?

    Absolutely. The design was conceived primarily for mobile devices, so its interface and button layout work perfectly on any size phone or tablet screen.

    Does it show the step-by-step procedure?

    That is our specialty! Beyond giving the final result, we provide a look at the intermediate steps, including carryovers and partial subtractions just as they are taught in school.

    Can I view the calculation history?

    Yes! At the top left of the blackboard frame, you will find the history button, which you can use to display or hide your previous operations.
